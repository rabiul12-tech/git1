Frequently Asked Questions

How does the farm share program work?

Our farm share program allows you to subscribe to regular deliveries of fresh, seasonal produce directly from our farm. You can choose between weekly or fortnightly deliveries, and even have a say in what crops we grow through our monthly voting system.

What's included in a typical farm box?

Each farm box contains 8+ varieties of seasonal produce, which may include leafy greens, root vegetables, fruits, and herbs. The exact contents vary based on seasonality and harvest conditions, but we always ensure a diverse and balanced selection.

Can I customize my farm box?

Yes! We offer customizable options for preferences and allergies. You can note your preferences in your account settings, and we'll do our best to accommodate them while working with what's in season.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to most urban areas in Malaysia. During signup, you can enter your postal code to check if we deliver to your area. We're continuously expanding our delivery routes to reach more communities.
